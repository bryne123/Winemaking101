Ingredients

Obviously this will vary greatly depending on what kind of wine you'd like to make, but for now we'll keep it simple:

  • 4L fruit juice
  • 800g Sugar
  • 200g raisins
  • 1 tsp wine yeast
  • 1 tsp betonite

Step 1: Sterilise

it's important to sterilise all the equipment you are using to prevent bacteria from growing in you brew, don't skip this step! Wash thoroughly with diluted bleach, vinegar or a crushed up campden tablet
